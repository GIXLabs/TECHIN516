# Need Assistance? We're Here to Help!

In our robotics lab course, encountering challenges is a part of the learning journey. We understand that issues can arise, and we are fully committed to helping you navigate and resolve them. Whether it's a technical glitch, a conceptual query, or something in between, our team is here to support you.

**Have You Checked Our FAQs?**
Before you reach out, take a peek at our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs). At each lab session, we have a set of FAQs tailored to the specific lab content. These FAQs might already have the answers to your questions, saving you time and giving you instant insights!

**How to Report Your Issue:**

We use GitHub to track issues, which helps us understand and address your concerns effectively. When you encounter a problem, please report it using our simplified format. Here’s how you can share your issue with us:

**Simple Issue Reporting Template:**

```
### Issue Title: [Lab Number] - Brief Description

**Lab Number:** (e.g., Lab 0, Lab 1, etc.)

**What Happened:**
Describe what you were doing and what went wrong. Include any error messages or unexpected outcomes. Feel free to combine what you expected to happen and what actually happened here.

**Visuals (if applicable):**
Attach screenshots or images that might help us understand your issue better. This could be error messages, robot setups, or anything visually relevant.

**Steps to Reproduce (if you can):**
Let us know what steps we can follow to see the issue ourselves. Include code snippets, commands, or setup details.

**What You've Tried:**
Have you tried any solutions or troubleshooting steps? Let us know what you’ve done so far.

### End of Report

```

**Don’t Worry About Perfection:**

Don't worry if you're not sure about how to fill out everything. Just give us a brief overview of the issue, and we'll work it out together!
